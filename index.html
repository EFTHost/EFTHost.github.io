Statutory Labour & Employment Law Compliance

We have expertise in statutory compliances of all the labour and employment laws which includes but not limited to:

  • The Employees' Provident Funds and Miscellaneous Provisions Act, 1952 (PF)
  • The Employees' State Insurance Act, 1948 (ESI)
  • The Factories Act, 1948
  • The Payment of Gratutity Act, 1972 ( Gratuity )
  • Code on Wages, 2019
  • Maternity Benefit Act, 1961
  • Saxual Harassment of Woemnt at Work Place ( Prevention , Prohibition and Redressal) Act, 2013
  • Building and other construction workers Act, 1996
   Scope of Services Related Compliance Management
    • Obtaining License/Registration under various Labour Laws
    • Obtaining PF Code No., ESI Code No.
    • Liaisoning with various Government Labour Departments/Officials
    • Labour law compliance audit
    • Periodical return filing
    • Statutory records maintenance
    • Preparation of monthly compliance i.e. PF Challan / ESI Challan etc.
    • Appearing before various labour authorities/labour courts
