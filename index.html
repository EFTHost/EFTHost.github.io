Services

Statutory Labour & Employment Law Compliance

We have expertise in statutory compliances of all the labour and employment laws which includes but not limited to

  • The Employees' Provident Funds and Miscellaneous Provisions Act, 1952 (PF)
  • The Employees' State Insurance Act, 1948 (ESI)
   Scope of Services Related Compliance Management
    • Obtaining License/Registration under various Labour Laws
    • Obtaining PF Code No., ESI Code No.

Documentation & Implementation of HR Policies

  • Designing the best H.R. after evaluating the company's H.R. requirements
  • Documenting all the H.R. policies and evaluating the same periodically
  • Help the company to implement the H.R. policies

Record keeping and Process Facilitation

  • Maintenance of Master Records of the employees
  • Employee Data & Records Maintenance
  • Employee Background Verification
  • Induction Process
  • Cost to Company (CTC) Designing
  • Training Records
  • Payroll Processing
  • Employee Performance Review and Rewards
